All materials are composed of grains, which inturn comprise many atoms. The visbility of these grains depends on their size. Conventional materials have grains varying in size from hundreds of microns to millimeters. Nanomaterials: The materials possessing grains of size ranging from 1 to 100 nm known as nanomaterials. which can be produced with different dimensionalities.
